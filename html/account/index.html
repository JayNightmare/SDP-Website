About

CKD Helper provides secure account management for both healthcare practitioners and patients. Our platform enables tracking of kidney health metrics over time, with features for storing historical eGFR calculations, viewing trends, and managing patient information. We prioritize data security and user privacy while making kidney health monitoring more accessible and organized.
